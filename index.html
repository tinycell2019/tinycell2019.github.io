About Tinycell

Tinycell is a mobile game studio specializing in casual and puzzle titles. We embrace AI tools across the team to work efficiently and deliver results faster. Our focus is to provide accessible, high-quality gameplay for a broad audience.

  • Built with Unity & Cocos
  • Casual & puzzle genres
  • Global launch-ready pipeline
